About Me

Design with craft, discipline, and curiosity.

My name is Rekha Nahata. Art has been my life since the very beginning.

I started my career as a Fashion Designer. After working for 4 years as a fashion designer and getting exposed to graphics media, I decided to learn art digitally. I completed a certificate program at Norwalk Community College in Graphic Design with a 4.0 GPA.

Working digitally is amazing and limitless. Through this program, I learned to use and combine different elements of Graphic Design for stronger visual outcomes. This portfolio showcases my work and abilities.
